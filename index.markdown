  <h1>Henrik Aalto</h1>

  <section class="about-section">
    <div class="intro-text">
      <p>Passionate product builder. <strong>IOI</strong> Silver Medalist, <strong>NCPC</strong> Champion, BSc Mathematics from Aalto University.</p>
      
      <p>I lead the Machine Learning team at Hamina Wireless, a top Finnish startup. We're building an exceptional product for wireless network design, validation, and troubleshooting. I've built and scaled machine learning pipelines to production.</p>
      
      <p>Published research on algorithmic efficiency in social networks. Wrote my thesis on classifying finite groups.</p>
      
      <p>I coach Finland's IOI team—developing problem sets, organizing training camps, and co-leading Team Finland at BOI 2023 and IOI 2025.</p>
