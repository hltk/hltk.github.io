About Henrik Aalto

I hold a BSc in Mathematics from Aalto University with a minor in Computer Science. I competed in the International Olympiad in Informatics (IOI) and the Baltic Olympiad in Informatics (BOI), earning medals in both.

Today I lead the Machine Learning team at Hamina Wireless, where I build and ship computer-vision solutions. Before that, I wrote RF physics simulation components and graphics engines here at Hamina.

I've published research on algorithmic efficiency in social-network influence and wrote my thesis on classifying finite groups. In my free time I volunteer as a coach for Finland's IOI Training Program.
